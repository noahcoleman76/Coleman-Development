About Knockout Promos

Welcome to Knockout Promos (previously Coleman Development), founded in 2024 in Utah County. We specialize in creating custom websites that reflect the unique identity and branding of each client.


Our mission is to provide top-tier web design services to businesses and entrepreneurs, helping to achieve their online aspirations.

Noah Coleman

Founder

"While living in Utah County, I noticed many of the local businesses my wife and I visited had little or no online presence. With a background in computer science, I realized I could help. I started reaching out to these businesses, offering my skills to build websites and improve their online visibility.
"What began as a small initiative turned into a growing niche. More and more businesses needed support, and I was ready to provide it. Today, Knockout Promos creates dynamic websites that bring in thousands of visitors each week, helping businesses thrive in the digital world."
Noah Coleman, Founder
